Common questions

What happens after I pay?
For the Launch package, your complete website files are delivered within 24 hours via email, ready to upload to any web host. For Managed and Premium, we take care of everything — you'll receive a link to your live site within 48 hours.
Do I need to do anything technical?
For Launch, minimal technical setup is required — we provide instructions and can point you to free hosting options. For Managed and Premium, you don't need to touch anything technical at all. We handle it.
Can I make changes to the site?
With Launch, you own the files and can edit them directly. With Managed and Premium, just send us what you need updated and we'll take care of it — usually within 24-48 hours.
What if I already have a hosting provider?
No problem. With the Launch package the files work on any host. With Managed we can either move you to our hosting or work with your existing provider — whatever is easier for you.
Is this a long-term contract?
Managed and Premium are billed annually but there's no multi-year commitment. If you decide to cancel, you keep the website files — you're never locked out of your own site.
What about the photography session?
The Premium package includes a professional photography session at your location. We'll photograph your team, facility, and work — all images are delivered in high resolution and integrated into your site. Scheduling is flexible and arranged after sign-up.
